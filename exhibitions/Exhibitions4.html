250 years of natural sciences

Description

FR NL ENG

In this fascinating hall, you will see and hear how unforgettable expeditions, unusual excavations and touching events led to a fantastic result: with 37 million specimens (minerals, dinosaurs, insects, shells, land and sea animals ...), the natural science collection of the Museum is the largest in Europe after those of Paris and London! Karel van Lotharingen probably didn't think that when he started his cabinet of curiosities in 1751.
