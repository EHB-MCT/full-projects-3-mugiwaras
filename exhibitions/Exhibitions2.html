FR NL ENG

Explore the evolution of man and his body in the "Gallery of Man - Our Evolution, Our Body". To start, fossils and 3D reconstructions guide you along the winding path of 7 million years of evolution.

Next, discover in an interactive way the adaptations that distinguish us from our ancestors (larger brains, smaller teeth...). Then delve into the wonderful workings of the complex machine that is our human body.

Uncover how your body constantly changes, how it works, what it needs to survive and reproduce (this can even interest your teenagers ;-))
