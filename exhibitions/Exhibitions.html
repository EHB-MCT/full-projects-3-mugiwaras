The Gallery of Dinosaurs is the largest hall in Europe dedicated entirely to dinosaurs. Dozens of specimens on an area of over 3000 square meters tell the story of their discovery, life and evolution...

The dinosaurs appeared at the end of the Triassic period, almost 230 million years ago. During the Jurassic period, they became more numerous and diverse and inhabited all continents. At the end of the Cretaceous period, 65 million years ago, they became extinct. But are they really gone? That's not so certain...

In the gallery, you will see how Olorotitan dies, learn how fossils are formed and challenge a virtual Pachycephalosaurus. Listen to the scream of Parasaurolophus, learn about cladistics, give each hadrosaur its own crest... Visit the Gallery of Dinosaurs with our Saura App or the Be My Guide podcasts...or both!
