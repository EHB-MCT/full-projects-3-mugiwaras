Mosasaurs

Description

FR NL ENG

When the tyrannosaurs ruled the mainland, giant flesh-eating lizards roamed the oceans: the mosasaurs. Interestingly, many have never heard of these imposing creatures. That will change after a visit to our Mosasaur Hall: it showcases a beautiful selection from our mosasaur collection, which belongs to the world's top. They are displayed in a clear and atmospheric hall, next to the iguanodon display in the mine.

One of the fossils of Belgian origin is the beautiful Hainosaurus bernardi, a mosasaur measuring 12.5 meters long! Admire the contemporaries of these powerful marine reptiles, compare their anatomy to that of reptiles and other living animals, discover how they swam and captured their prey...
