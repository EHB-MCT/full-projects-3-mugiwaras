Gallery of Evolution

Description

FR NL ENG

With giant steps through the history of life ... In the Gallery of Evolution, you will flash through billions of years of earthly existence. The gallery focuses on 6 important chapters of evolution: the Cambrian explosion, the rich aquatic life of the Devonian, the conquest of land during the Carboniferous, the tumultuous seas of the Jurassic, the rise of mammals in the Eocene, and the impact of humans in the present.

Follow the fate of life on earth, from primitive pioneers to sea monsters to humans ... and even a glimpse into the future.
